ls - list directory contents



Listing with year
=================
Use --full-time argumnt.

Example:
$ ls --full-time
total 8
-rwx------ 1 remarcil dnr.webmaster 2277 2019-05-23 \
15:42:57.443335000 -0800 dnratwcftest.dnr.state.ak.us.csr
-rwx------ 1 remarcil dnr.webmaster 3247 2019-05-23 \
15:42:57.450337000 -0800 dnratwcftest.dnr.state.ak.us.key
$
